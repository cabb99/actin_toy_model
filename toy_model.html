Actin bundle toy model

Bead-chain actin filaments on a hexagonal lattice. Bonds keep monomer spacing, curvature penalizes bending, and crosslinker springs connect nearest-neighbor filaments every ~35.5 nm.

Energy terms:
bonds: ½kb(|rm+1−rm|−b)²
bending: ½kθ|rm+1−2rm+rm−1|²
crosslinks: ½kcl(|ri−rj|−a)² + ½k⊥Δz²
